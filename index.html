Q: How do I get there?

Tram routes 16 (from Melbourne Central/Flinders St) and 96 (from Southern Cross) stop at Canterbury Rd outside The Play Lounge. Other tram routes 3, 5, 64 and 67 stop at St Kilda Junction, around a 12 minute walk from the Play Lounge.

Street parking along Fitzroy St is limited, but there are paid parking options in Albert Park Reserve along Lakeside Drive (free after 9pm), behind the Prince of Wales Hotel off Acland St or along Jacka Boulevard.
